Privacy Policy

Effective: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information AI While You Sleeping ("AWYS", "we", "us") collects when you use our platform, how we use it, and the choices you have. AWYS helps creators generate AI short-form videos and publish them to the social media accounts they connect (including TikTok, YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook).

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information. Your email address and any profile information you provide when signing up.
  • Connected-account data. When you connect a third-party account (such as TikTok), we receive the OAuth access and refresh tokens that you authorize, along with basic profile information (display name, avatar, open ID) returned by that platform. We only request the scopes needed to deliver the features you use.
  • Content you generate. Scripts, visuals, captions, and rendered videos produced through the service, along with the trending topics and prompts used to produce them.
  • Publishing activity. Records of which pieces of content you chose to publish, to which connected accounts, and the response returned by each platform.
  • Technical data. Standard server logs such as IP address, browser type, and timestamps for security and debugging purposes.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide the service: generating content on request, and publishing it to the connected accounts you explicitly authorize.
  • To maintain the security and reliability of the platform.
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To improve AWYS (for example, to debug errors or evaluate model output quality). We do not sell your personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

AWYS integrates with third-party platforms to deliver its core features. When you connect one of these, information is shared with that platform according to its own privacy policy:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • YouTube / Google Privacy Policy
  • Instagram Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Privacy Policy

AWYS also uses AI model providers (such as Anthropic for script generation, ElevenLabs for voiceover synthesis, and Replicate for video generation) to process prompts and generate content. These providers act as subprocessors and do not receive your connected-account tokens.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. OAuth tokens are stored only while your connection to that platform is active and are deleted when you disconnect the platform or close your account. Generated content is retained so that you can review or re-publish it, and can be deleted at your request.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • Revoke platform access. You can disconnect any third-party account at any time from within AWYS or directly in the platform's app settings (e.g., TikTok Settings → Security → Manage app permissions).
  • Access, correction, deletion. You may request a copy of your data, ask us to correct inaccuracies, or delete your account and associated data by emailing us at the address below.
  • Opt-out. You can stop using AWYS at any time; upon closing your account we will delete your stored tokens and personal data within a reasonable period, subject to legal retention obligations.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including encrypted transport (HTTPS) and limited internal access to credentials. No system is perfectly secure, and we encourage you to use a strong password and to disconnect accounts you no longer use.

7. Children

AWYS is not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact
